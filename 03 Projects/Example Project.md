## Notes

This vault is built for ADHD-shaped workflows: alternating between deep focus ("OH BOI mode") and low-energy days. The Daily template asks you to log which mode you're in. Over time the Weekly note shows the rhythm.

The Dataview queries in the templates auto-roll up your tasks. You don't have to manually track. Just write tasks anywhere with `- [ ]` and they show up in the right places.
